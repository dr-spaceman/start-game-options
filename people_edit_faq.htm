Videogam.in / Contributing to People Pages

All Videogam.in registered users have the ability to add and edit information about videogame creators listed in the People Database.

Table of Contents
  1. Adding a new person to the database
  2. Adding new information to an existing person
  3. Editing existing information about a person

Adding a new person to the database

Create a new profile page by first crediting that person as a developer for a specific game. You can do so navigating to a game page and clicking "Contribute Something" or "Add a Developer" in the far right page column.

Adding new information to an existing person

To add the following information, click "Edit this Page" in the top right corner of the person's profile:

  • Personal details including birthdate, birthplace, and nationality
  • Title/Profession
  • Aliases or foreign-language names
  • Companies and Development Groups
  • Profile Picture

To add a biography, click "Submit one" in the biography field. You will then be taken to the wiki page for this person's biography. Like other wiki fields such as game synopses and game developer descriptions, all edits to biographies are stored in a databank for future reference and protection from vandalism.

To add a new credit to a game or music album, navigate to that game or music album's overview page and click "Contribute Something" or "Add a Developer" in the far right page column.

To add additional photographs, begin a new post session or click "Post News, Blogs, or other Content" on the person's profile page. In order for your pictures to appear on the profile page, your post needs to have the following characteristics:
  • Content Type: Image or Gallery
  • Media Type: Photos
  • Category: Content/Archive
  • Finally, after you have submitted your new post, tag this person's name
If and when this post is approved for publication, the uploaded pictures will appear under the person's biography.

Only decent quality, large images should be uploaded in this case. Please refrain from uploading images of poor quality or small size (less than 300 pixels in width).

You can also add other Pictures, Artwork, and Print Media via the same method as above.

Related News Articles and Blog Posts can be tagged with this person's name in order to be shown on their profile page.

Add Links related to this person by clicking "Add a Link" under the links section, or by beginning a new post session. In order for the link to appear in the links section, your post must have the following characteristics:
  • Content Type: Link
  • Category: Content/Archive
  • Finally, after you have submitted your new post, tag this person's name

Quotes said by that person can be added via the same method as a Link, only with Content Type: Quote.

Similarly, Videos of that person (hosted on another website like Youtube) can be posted with Content Type: Video.

Editing existing information about a person

By clicking "Edit This Page" in the top right corner of a person's profile, you can edit any of the following data:
  • Personal details including birthdate, birthplace, and nationality
  • Title/Profession
  • Aliases or foreign-language names
  • Companies and Development Groups
  • Profile Picture
  • Roles and Notes for credited work on Games and Music Albums

To edit a biography, hover your mouse over the biography text. A link should then appear in the bottom-right corner of the text.

In order to edit News, Blogs, and other Post Content (including Links, Quotes, Pictures, and Videos), you'll need to go to the permalink page of each individual post. However, if you didn't post the item, you can't edit it unless you are an administrator. For your own posted items, you can browse and manage them on your Posts Management Page.
